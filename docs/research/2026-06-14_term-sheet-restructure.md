## Executive summary

The original term sheet describes **commercial economics** Hanover and Kao want inside a **Delaware LLC JV closing simultaneously** with land, GMP, loan, and permit. That closing choreography **conflicts with §1031** (LLC membership ≠ like-kind real property; promote inside the exchange vehicle). We will submit a **restructured term sheet** that **preserves commercial terms** (95/5, promote, fees, guaranties, overrun split) while **splitting legal documents and timing** to accommodate a §1031 path (EAT/build-to-suit, then JV or TIC — structure still **OPEN**). **Hanover Industrial LLC** remains Sponsor; **Hanover Construction Group** stays a **GMP contractor**, not a JV equity holder. The Simi Valley org chart shows the **LP/GP + land SPV pattern**; Construction Group is **off the equity chart** per term sheet.
