# Project Goal

**Internal working model only** — not client-facing. Read this before any edit.

---

## What we are building

An **accurate representation** of what the legal and economic structure **might** look like for the full Belara → North Forsyth transaction — every step, every party, every dollar — so our family can evaluate paths to a **§1031 deferral** before entities are formed or terms are signed.

Accuracy beats confidence. Unverified claims get labeled **DRAFT** or **OPEN**.

---

## Deliverables (site tabs)

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Start Here** | Plain-English §1031 primer + acronym legend + cast + the core tension — for a deal participant with **no** exchange background; makes the site self-contained |
| **Overview** | End-to-end summary: deal facts, the C-Hybrid structure, bifurcation, money flow, clocks, boot risk, seasoning, entity map, ranked risks |
| **Kao** | Our family's role — exchanger steps, entity alignment, constraints, what we must not do |
| **Hanover** | Developer's role — four Phase-1 roles, control line, Phase-2 bifurcation, conduct (all **negotiable**) |
| **Strake** | Belara buyer's place in the flow — for **our** understanding of the closing/QI wire; not a document we send them |
| **Sources** | Statute, regulations, rev procs, case law — cited, each paired with its role |

Structure detail may live in Overview (current site) or a future dedicated tab — whatever keeps the story consistent.

---

## What "accurate" means

For each step document:

1. **Timing** — Day 0, 45, 180, post-close, exit (Belara target close: **Aug 15–30, 2026**, flexible — **our side has more leverage**)
2. **Parties** — who acts, holds title, signs (entities are **placeholders** until formed closer to closing)
3. **Money** — sources and uses; no hand-waving
4. **Requirements** — §1031 clocks, constructive receipt, in-place value by Day 180, etc.

Never state an assumption as fact.

---

## Structural objective

Find a path to defer the Belara gain into North Forsyth while keeping **economic risk** reasonably aligned with the Hanover term sheet economics (95/5, promote, fees, guaranties).

**Hanover has not agreed to anything yet.** JV timing and promote shape are directionally understood but **fully negotiable**. **Our family is flexible** on structure to achieve a defensible 1031.

**No structure is locked.** Competing models (TIC at Day 180, Structure A exchange-then-§721, others) stay **OPEN** until source docs and advisors confirm.

**Outside advisors have not evaluated any structure yet.** This site is our working draft.
